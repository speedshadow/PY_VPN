FAQ

What is a VPN?

A VPN (Virtual Private Network) encrypts your internet traffic and hides your IP address, improving your privacy and security online.

Why should I use a VPN?

VPNs help you bypass geo-restrictions, access blocked content, and protect your data from hackers and ISPs.

Are VPNs legal?

VPN usage is legal in most countries, but always check your local laws before using one.

How do I choose the best VPN?

Look for VPNs with strong privacy policies, fast speeds, global servers, and features like no-logs and kill switch. See our reviews for the best options.
